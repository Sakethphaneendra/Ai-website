Top 10 mind-blowing free AI tools that anyone can use in 2023 will save you time and make your life easier. The future is now! with the rapid advancements in artificial intelligence (AI), it’s no surprise that numerous amazing AI tools are already available to people, and many more are sure to come in the next few years. Here we listed the mind-blowing free AI tools that will make your life easier.

GFP-GAN - Photo Restoration Tool: GFP-GAN or Generative Facial Prior-Generative Adversarial Network is a free AI tool that helps to fix old photos instantly within seconds. It restores old and faded photos surprisingly in a realistic way in seconds. It was developed by Tencent researchers by taking the information from two AI models and merging them to fill in a photo’s missing details. It replaces the missing details with the most realistic details in seconds. It creates a good balance between realness and fidelity.

Notion.ai — Copy Writing Tool: The notion.ai is a copywriting tool to create high-quality plagiarism-free content. It helps you assist in writing, brainstorming, editing, and summarizing the content. All you need to do is to tell the app what you want to write such as a blog post, social media post, press release, or job description. It even helps you create poems and outlines for any subject or topic.

visily.ai --UI Wireframing & Prototyping Tool: Visily is a powerful software that utilizes AI to help teams craft stunning app wireframes with ease. With its advanced AI capabilities and extensive UI library, Visily can seamlessly convert hand-drawn sketches or even existing app screenshots into sleek, editable wireframes in just moments. The result? Professional-grade designs without the hassle of manual creation. Why spend hours perfecting your wireframes when Visily can do it for you in mere minutes? Give Visily a try and unlock the potential of effortless design!

Copy.ai – Copywriting Tool The copy.ai is also a free AI tool that helps your team with all kinds of copywriting needs such as writing product descriptions, ad copies, email writing, blogs, video content, and website copies. This tool generates unique and easy-to-read content that engages your readers. Copy.ai is also ideal for writing social media captions, Facebook content, start-up ideas, and many more.

JADBio — AutoML: JADBio is a user-friendly machine-learning platform equipped with powerful knowledge extraction tools that helps students to dive into machine learning easily. Although it is built purposely for multi-omics and biotech, you can still upload any sort of curated data and analyze it. The tool also helps people to build a predictive model. And the best part of this tool is it offers a lifetime free account.
